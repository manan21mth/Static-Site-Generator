Yoga Day

written by Anand Kumar . on 21-06-2023

The International Day of Yoga has been celebrated annually on June 21 since 2015, following its inception in the United Nations General Assembly in 2014. The Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi suggested the date of June 21 as it is the longest day of the year in the Northern Hemisphere and shares a special significance in many parts of the world.
